# PaisaBazaar Customer & Loan Analysis Dashboard

## Overview

This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard built using PaisaBazaar customer and loan data. The dashboard provides valuable insights into customer demographics, income distribution, loan patterns, credit scores, credit card usage, interest rates, and payment behaviour.

The objective of this project is to transform raw financial data into meaningful visual insights that support data-driven decision-making in the banking and financial services domain.

## Business Objective

The primary objective of this dashboard is to analyze customer financial characteristics and loan-related behavior to identify trends, understand customer segments, and support financial decision-making.

The dashboard enables stakeholders to explore customer demographics, income levels, credit scores, loan distribution, and payment patterns through interactive visualizations and filters.

---

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

The dashboard includes four key performance indicators that provide a quick overview of customer and loan-related metrics:

* Total Customers
* Average Age
* Average Annual Income
* Average Interest Rate

These KPIs help users understand the overall customer profile and financial landscape at a glance.

---

## Dashboard Features

### Interactive Slicers

The dashboard includes dynamic dropdown slicers that allow users to perform detailed analysis across different customer segments.

* Credit Score
* Occupation
* Age Group

The slicers interact with all visualizations and KPI cards, enabling customized exploration of the data.

### Visualizations

The dashboard consists of seven interactive visualizations:

1. Age vs Income Analysis
2. Interest Rate Analysis
3. Average Annual Income by Occupation
4. Loan Distribution Analysis
5. Payment Behaviour Analysis
6. Credit Score Distribution
7. Credit Cards Analysis

These visualizations provide a comprehensive understanding of customer demographics, financial behavior, and loan-related trends.

## Key Insights

* The average customer age is approximately 33 years, indicating a predominantly young and working-age customer base.
* Standard credit score customers represent the largest customer segment.
* Most customers maintain between two and four active loans.
* Income levels vary significantly across occupations.
* Payment behaviour patterns reveal different customer spending habits and financial preferences.
* Interest rates are distributed across multiple customer groups, providing insights into borrowing trends.
* Credit card ownership patterns help understand customer financial activity and credit utilization.
